Added Jaina Proudmoore the mage from Hearthstone. Including 75 cards, 9 new relics and 9 unique potions.
This mod is developing and needs to be optimized. Wish to receive feedbacks on balancing and bugs. And this will be continually updating, thanks!
New mechanics:
Three spell genres: Frost, Fire, Arcane, each with its own characteristics.
Freeze: Frozen enemies may not act, use the powerful freezing mechanism to control the combat!
Combustion: Enemies with combustion will take direct HP damage, let the raging fire devour your enemies!
Spell Force: Increases attack damage and block, may also enhance other effects.

炉石传说 法师吉安娜Mod

加入了《炉石传说》中的法师——吉安娜·普罗德摩尔。包括75张卡牌，9个全新的遗物，9种独特的药水。
全新机制：
三种法术流派：冰霜、火焰、奥术，每个流派都有自己的特点。
冻结：被冻结的敌人无法行动，用强大的冻结机制来掌控战局吧！
燃烧：具有燃烧的敌人会直接受到生命值伤害，让熊熊烈火吞噬你的敌人吧！
法术强度：提高攻击伤害和格挡，还有其他特殊的效果
